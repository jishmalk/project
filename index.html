About

Nike, Inc., founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, has a rich history in athletic footwear and apparel. Here are some key points:
1.Origins: Nike began as Blue Ribbon Sports in 1964. It officially became Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971. The company’s name is inspired by Nike, the Greek goddess of victory.
2.First Commercial Design: Nike’s first commercial design was the Cortez, a cushioned shoe for running. It gained cultural significance and was famously worn by Tom Hanks in the movie Forrest Gump.
3.Air Jordan Line: In 1985, Nike introduced the iconic Air Jordan line in collaboration with basketball legend Michael Jordan.
4.Slogan and Brand Value: The famous “Just Do It” slogan was launched in 1988. By 2023, Nike’s brand value exceeded $74 billion.
